About

I'm Founding ML Engineer at Kipo AI. I completed my MS in Scientific Computing at Penn (4.0 GPA), working with Andrea Liu on learning in physical systems and Mark Liberman at the LDC on NLP. I also interned at Cisco Research.

Previously: At Microsoft Research India, I worked on Project Froid (scalar UDF inlining for MSSQL) with Karthik Ramachandra. At RIKEN Japan, I worked on open quantum systems with Franco Nori and Neill Lambert, co-developing QuTiP-BoFiN. At J.P. Morgan, I was a Quantitative Researcher on the Rates desk, building pricing and risk models. At tensoralpha (crypto hedge fund), I worked on crypto derivatives trading strategies with Thomas Schmelzer. I hold a BS in Computer Science from BITS Pilani.

I work on understanding how neural networks learn and reason: geometry of memory in RNNs, multi-step reasoning in LLMs, learning in physical systems.
